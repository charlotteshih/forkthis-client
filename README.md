# ForkThis!
A Sleek & Simple App For Storing Your Recipes!

## Concept
With **ForkThis!**, users can jot down recipes both old and new to an easy-to-use interface and organize them into relevant folders for easy perusal.  **ForkThis!** takes the scrolling out of recipe-hunting; no more 4,000-page essays on food blogs before arriving at the recipe â€” just a laser focus on getting you the information you want, when you want it. Whether you're a professional chef or an amateur home cook, **ForkThis!** has got everything you need so you can focus on what you do best: making yummy food not just today, but every meal, every day!
